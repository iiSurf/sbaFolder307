Dr. Brie Everard, PsyD, LP

Psychologist

My style is influenced by the work of Brené Brown, who writes, "We are all made of strength and struggle."I will encourage you to acknowledge and build on your strengths and support you through your struggles. I ampassionate about helping people move towards self-acceptance and away from shame and self-doubt. I strive tobe authentic, compassionate, and respectful, and to foster a relationship where you feel genuinely seen,heard, and understood. I work well with adolescents and adults who are grappling with depression; anxiety; transitions; lowself-esteem; relational and attachment concerns; trauma; grief and loss; and identity shifts. I strongly believe in the healing power of play and creativity, and infuse my work with humor, spontaneity, and imagination. I am trained in many styles of therapy, from expressive arts to clinical, "evidence-based" styles like CBT. We will work together to find the most effective approach for you.

Dr. Asha Madsen-Humeniuk, PsyD, LP

Psychologist

I am a Washington stated licensed psychologist (license number: PY61058287), telehealth provider inFlorida, and PsyPact member who works with children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families. I adapt mytherapy to each individual and utilize several therapeutic approaches including: person-centered,cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy (TF-CBT), parent-childinteraction therapy (PCIT), strength-based, family systems, emotion focused therapy (EFT), and EMDR.

Life can get difficult, and sometimes a little help from a professional can make things better. I careabout your well-being and want to provide you all the tools you need to explore, process, and share in anon-judgmental, respectful, and safe environment.
